About Me

Ahh, hello! I am Kevin Xiao, an engineer of software, a developer of programs and a writer of code. I also happen to be a student at the University of Waterloo, studying the art and the craft of software engineering. What I've learned is that I love it all. From front-end web to server-side data to low-level drivers, I absolutely love the learning, the process and the challenge. I've picked up programming contests, hackathons and personal projects to keep myself busy with coding, but I'm absolutely looking to start a few new things. Keep an eye on my GitHub repo for interesting new projects I'll start to pick up.

Apart from coding, I'm a huge computer enthusiast. I build and modify my own computer hardware, tweak the software and firmware to my own liking and proceed to use the heck out of it. That, of course, includes some serious gaming. Whether it's League of Legends or Starcraft or Diablo or Overwatch, I do try to take some time to enjoy myself in a virtual world of my choosing. However, when staying indoors just doesn't agree with me anymore, I will often run the court for a bit of light pick-up basketball (admittedly I'm not particularly good) or hit the slopes for some quality snowboarding.
